Load HEX file Flash Firmware

Instructions:
1. Connect your TX or RX module via usb to serial dongle to the computer (with DTR)
2. Click on "Load HEX file" to load the .hex file you just downloaded
3. Press "Flash Firmware" and wait for process to finish
4. You are done

Optional: Check "Erase EEPROM" checkbox if you are upgrading to a newer version of firmware.
