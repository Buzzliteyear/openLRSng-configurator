Voltage Warning

Please note that only certain openLRS units can operate with 5V USB-serial adapter.
It is strongly suggested to always use 3.3V USB-serial adapter unless you know what you are doing.

Permanent damage is caused immediately by powering non compatible units with 5V.

How to connect:
1. connect your serial to usb dongle to the transmitter module (leave the DTR pin disconnected)
2. enter binding mode on your transmitter module (power down the transmitter, hold down the button on the module and power up)
3. connect with this utility.

Note: if you can't disconnect DTR (hard wired/soldered), add a few seconds of timeout before connecting (select box next to baud rate)
And don't forget to hold the button for that duration of time so module actually enters binding mode

Flashing and updating firmware:
1. you can use the build in firmware upload utility (you can download specific .hex files in openlrsng repo)
2. or you can flash the respective firmware through Arduino IDE
