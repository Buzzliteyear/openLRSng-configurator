Voltage Warning

Please note that only certain openLRS units can operate with 5V USB-serial adapter.
It is strongly suggested to always use 3.3V USB-serial adapter.

Permanent damage is caused immediately by powering non compatible units with 5V.

Optional USB Permissions

Due to the nature of arduino leonardo bootloader design, Configurator requires additional USB permissions to be able to restart Atmega32u4 based hardware.
