How to connect:
1. connect your serial to usb dongle to the transmitter module (leave the DTR pin disconnected)
2. enter binding mode on your transmitter module (power down the transmitter, hold down the button on the module and power up)
3. connect with this utility.

Flashing and updating firmware:
1. you can use the build in firmware upload utility (you can download specific .hex files in openlrsng repo)
2. or you can flash the respective firmware through Arduino IDE
