Voltage Warning

Please note that only certain openLRS units can operate with 5V USB-serial adapter.
It is strongly suggested to always use 3.3V USB-serial adapter unless you know what you are doing.

Permanent damage is caused immediately by powering non compatible units with 5V.

How to connect (with auto-connect enabled):
1. Start the application.
2. Plug in your module to the PC
Note: Software will automatically find the serial port and try to connect

How to connect (with auto-connect disabled):
1. Start the application.
2. Plug in your module to the PC
3. New serial port should be automatically selected for you
4. Click Connect

Flashing and updating firmware:
1. you can use the build in firmware upload utility
2. or you can flash the respective firmware through Arduino IDE
